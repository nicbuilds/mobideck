Premium Species

Scientifically proven durability. Artistically selected grains.

Cedro (Cedar)

Highly valued for its natural **thujaplicins**—chemical compounds that serve as natural fungicides.

Janka: 900 lbf Stable

Maculí

Remarkably resistant to fungal decay and termites. Ideal for luxury exterior surfaces.

Origin: Tropical Am. Lustrous

Tzalam

The "Mayan Walnut" is highly resistant to rot and moisture, superior for heavy-traffic decks.

Janka: 1,400 lbf Heavy Duty

Teca (Teak)

High percentage of natural oils and silica content make it virtually waterproof and self-protecting.

Grade: Maritime Elite

Parota

Extreme weight-to-strength ratio. Lighter than other hardwoods with striking golden-brown edges.

Eco: Sustainable Wide Plank
