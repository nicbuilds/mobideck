Premium Species

A comparative guide to the world's most durable and aesthetically superior timbers.

Cedro (Cedar)

Spanish Cedar is a marvel of biological engineering. Renowned for its aromatic scent, it contains high concentrations of natural thujaplicins. These chemical compounds act as a built-in defense mechanism, making the wood naturally toxic to decay-causing fungi and wood-boring insects.

Hardness900 lbf (Janka)
StabilityHigh (Low Warping)
Color RangeLight Pink to Red-Brown
DurabilityExcellent (Fungicide Content)
Best UseRailings & Pergolas

Maculí

Scientifically labeled *Tabebuia rosea*, Maculí is a high-performance hardwood with a stunning visual profile. It is prized for its straight-to-irregular grain pattern that provides a high-luster finish. Beyond its beauty, it is technically sound, offering a robust resistance to tropical decay.

Hardness1,100 lbf (Estimated)
TextureFine to Medium
Color RangePale Brown to Golden
ResilienceHigh (Termite Proof)
OriginTropical America

Tzalam

Commonly known as "Mayan Walnut," Tzalam is the gold standard for high-density outdoor decking. Its heartwood features a dramatic, deep chocolate hue with occasional plum streaks. Its density makes it incredibly tough against physical wear and heavy foot traffic.

Hardness1,400 lbf (Janka)
DensityVery High
Color RangeDeep Chocolate/Plum
MaintenanceLow (Natural Luster)
Best UsePremium Decking

Teca (Teak)

Teak is the undisputed king of maritime timber. It produces a natural oily resin that acts as a permanent waterproofing agent. Even in harsh saltwater environments, Teak maintains its structural integrity without the need for constant chemical treatment or sealing.

Hardness1,070 lbf (Janka)
SilicaSelf-Protecting Content
Color RangeGolden to Dark Honey
GradeElite Maritime
WeatheringExcellent (Zero Rot)

Parota

Parota (*Enterolobium cyclocarpum*) is favored for its massive scale and sustainability. It offers a unique weight-to-strength ratio, being significantly lighter than other hardwoods while providing immense structural stability. It is famous for its wide, live-edge slabs.

GrainInterlocked
WeightLighter Profile
AestheticWide-Plank / Live Edge
Eco RatingFast Growing
StrengthHigh Ratio
